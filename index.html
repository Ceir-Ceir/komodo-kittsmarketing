Do you build it for me or is it DIY?
DIY. Fully customizable to your workflow. Use our templates and patterns, connect your tools, and shape Komodo around your offers and delivery.
Is Komodo suitable for non‑tech‑savvy agents?
Yes. We deliver a done‑for‑you build with white‑glove onboarding and training. The interface is clean and opinionated, and our team actively supports you so you can focus on clients—not configuration.
What tools can Komodo replace?
Notion (docs/tasks), Airtable/CRM, Calendly (scheduling), n8n/Zapier (automations), and Client Portal apps — all centralized with analytics.
How does the client portal work?
Each client gets a secure portal with real‑time status of automations, open tasks, deliverables, and analytics. You control visibility and branding.
Do you use n8n/Zapier/Node‑Red — how is this different?
We implement with best‑fit tooling (often n8n/Node‑Red + native APIs). Komodo unifies CRM, ops, and automations into one opinionated system we maintain with you.
How long to go live?
Most teams ship an MVP in 1–2 weeks, then iterate weekly. Migrations or complex automations can add time; we plan timelines with you up front.

Can you migrate my existing data and flows?
Yes. We import contacts, deals, docs, and replicate key automations. We’ll map sources, clean data, and verify everything before switching over.
Who owns my data? How is it secured?
You own your data. We deploy with hardened defaults, access controls, audit trails, encrypted secrets, and regular backups. We’ll align with your compliance needs.
I’m solo now — will Komodo scale as I hire?
Absolutely. Roles, permissions, and workflows support contractors and teams. Start simple, add pipelines and automations as you grow.
